1. Revenue Segmentation (SQL Analysis)
Gender Gap: Male customers contributed significantly more revenue ($157,890) compared to Female customers ($75,191).

Loyalty: Over 80% of the customer base (3,116 shoppers) are classified as Loyal, indicating high brand trust but a low conversion rate to paid subscriptions.

Age Performance: The "Young" demographic is the most profitable, leading both in total revenue volume and sales density.

2. Behavioral Patterns (Python & Power BI)
Subscription Opportunity: Only 27% of shoppers are current subscribers. 73% of the high-frequency "Loyal" group remains unsubscribed.

Category Dominance: Clothing is the primary revenue driver, followed by Accessories.

Average Order Value (AOV): Maintains a consistent average of $59.76 across most segments.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations
Subscription Conversion: Implement a "Subscriber-Only Early Access" program to convert the 73% non-subscriber base.

Demographic Targeting: Prioritize marketing spend on the Young-Male segment, which shows the highest ROI.

Shipping Incentives: Leverage Express Shipping as a reward for high-value carts (orders >$75) to increase the overall AOV.
